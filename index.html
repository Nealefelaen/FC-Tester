Welcome to the FC Tester page

It's pretty simple to use, change the settings below to the values you want and then press the 'Next' button. When you're ready to go press the 'Go!' button, the 'Next' button will be ready under your cursor. After you've started, press the 'Next' button once you recall the FCs image, the time it took you to recall it will be recorded and the next FC will be shown. After all of the FCs have been shown the number of times set below you will be taken to a statistics page that will show your results.
Good Luck!
